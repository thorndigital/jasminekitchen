Our Story

A Passion for Authentic Food

Jasmine Kitchen is the only Mediterranean restaurant in the Eastside area (Kirkland, Redmond, Bellevue) that offers authentic Mediterranean rotisserie chicken. Our journey began in 2016 with a simple mission: to share the rich, vibrant flavors of our homeland with our new community.

We offer delicious Gyros, Falafel, Shawarma, Kebabs, and Salads, with all our food being halal. We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's about family, tradition, and creating memories. Every dish we prepare is a piece of our heritage, made with love and the freshest ingredients available.

Thank you for letting us share our passion with you.
